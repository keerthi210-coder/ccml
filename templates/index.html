About Coimbatore City Municipal Corporation (CCMC)

CCMC is the governing body responsible for the administration and development of Coimbatore city. Through its Knowledge & Study Centres, CCMC provides free competitive exam coaching, skill development programs, and training sessions — creating accessible opportunities for every citizen to advance their career.
