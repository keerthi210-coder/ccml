Who We Are

Coimbatore City Municipal Corporation

The Coimbatore City Municipal Corporation (CCMC) is the governing body responsible for the administration, infrastructure, and overall development of Coimbatore city. Committed to enhancing quality of life, CCMC focuses on delivering efficient public services and promoting inclusive urban growth.

Through its Knowledge & Study Centres and Padaipagam creative learning spaces, CCMC facilitates competitive examination coaching, skill development programs, and creative activity sessions — creating accessible opportunities for every citizen.
